# CeXaR Medical Claims Policy

CeXaR is a medical AI research project. All claims must be bounded by evidence.

## Allowed Language

Use careful language such as:

- "In this experiment..."
- "On this dataset split..."
- "Under these preprocessing assumptions..."
- "The model produced..."
- "This suggests, but does not prove..."
- "Requires clinical validation..."

## Forbidden Claims Without Evidence

Do not claim:

- The model is clinically validated
- The model is diagnostic-ready
- The model is safer than a clinician
- The model reaches radiologist-level performance
- A heatmap proves causal reasoning
- High AUROC means clinical usefulness

## Required Limitations

Reports must mention relevant limitations:

- Dataset shift
- Patient leakage risk
- Label noise
- Class imbalance
- Calibration uncertainty
- Shortcut learning
- Heatmap fidelity limitations
- Missing external validation
- Human-in-the-loop validation needs
